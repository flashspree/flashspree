About Us

In the year 2020, we realized that a lot of people have lost their jobs and were forced to look for new means to survive. Thus, many decided to open a home business selling various products from food to arts and crafts. Unfortunately, most of these home businesses do not have the experience to take professional picture of their products to market their business. Separately, schools were closed and photography students were left with limited to no photohunting opportunities. This is where we came in. We are fascinated by the idea of being able to connect business needs and studentsâ€™ passion for photography.

Flashspree exists with the sole purpose of facilitating both student photographers and home business owners to set a photoshoot as convenient as possible. While there are so many ways to write about your product, a picture is worth a thousand words.

Our Mission

To create a platform easily used by both student photographers and business owners and alieviate the impact of Covid-19 both stakeholders

Our Vision

Bridging and helping the needs of home business owner through product photography, where it is available at gratuitous and utmost convenience.
